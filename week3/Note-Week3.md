What is the probability of $P(S_n=n)$ ?
Lets:
$$
P(S_n​=n)=P(X_1​=1,X_2​=1,…,X_n​=1)
$$
So:
$$
P(S_n​=n)=p^n=(\frac{1}{2})^n
$$
Formula:
$$
P(S_n​=k)=\binom{k}{n}​p^k(1−p)^{n−k}
$$
